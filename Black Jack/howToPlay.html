How to Play Blackjack

Objective

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer's hand, without going over 21 (busting).

Card Values

  • Number Cards (2-10): Face value
  • Face Cards (J, Q, K): Worth 10 points each
  • Ace (A): Worth 11 or 1 point (player decides)

Game Flow

  1. Place Your Bet - Before cards are dealt
  2. Deal: You and the dealer each get 2 cards face-up (dealer's second card is hidden)
  3. Your Turn: Choose to Hit, Stand, Double Down, or Split
  4. Dealer's Turn: Dealer reveals their hole card and follows strict rules (must hit on 16 or less, stand on 17 or more)
  5. Determine Winner: Closest to 21 without busting wins

Your Actions

  • Hit: Request another card to increase your hand value
  • Stand: Take no more cards; compare your hand to the dealer's

Winning Outcomes

  • Blackjack (Natural 21): Ace + 10-value card on first two cards (usually pays 1.5x your bet)
  • Regular Win: Your hand beats the dealer's hand (pays 1x your bet)
  • Bust: Your hand exceeds 21 (you lose)

Basic Strategy Tips

  • Hit on 16 or less; stand on 17 or more
  • If dealer shows 4, 5, or 6, be cautious (dealer likely to bust)

Example Hand

You: King (10) + 6 = 16

Dealer (showing): 7

Action: Hit (dealer likely to make 17-21)

Outcome: You get a 5 → Total 21 → You Win!
